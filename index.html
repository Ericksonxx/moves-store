📦

How it works

Three steps.
Total visibility.

1
You create the campaign
Set up your campaign in the dashboard — define installation steps and upload reference photos so employees know exactly what the finished result should look like.
Brand dashboard
2
Store employees execute on their phone
Each store receives a unique link. They open it on any phone — no app download needed. They follow the guided steps and upload photos with automatic GPS tagging.
Store PWA · no install needed
3
You see everything in real time
Your dashboard updates live as stores complete each step. Alerts surface problems immediately. At the end, you have a complete photographic record ready to export.

Features

Multi-store campaign management
